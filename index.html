WILDLIFE

The diverse habitats and climates of Red Dead Redemption 2 are home to around 200 species of animals, birds and fish, all of which behave and respond to their environment in a unique way.

HUNTING & FISHING

Hunting and fishing are essential skills for survival in the wilderness, providing food, materials and a source of income.

Hunting wildlife provides valuable natural resources like meat, hides and other useful materials.

Game can be found across a range of habitats. Follow clues like blood to track it down.

Selecting the proper weapon is important to the quality and yield from your kill.

Like hunting, fishing also yields meat and useful materials.

HORSES

Horses are essential companions in the wilderness, providing transportation and carrying your gear.

Wild horses can be found in the Heartlands among other habitats.

Buy, sell and store horses from Stables, or purchase provisions to keep your horse fed.

Your bond with your horse represents trust and understanding.

Store weapons, outfits, masks and hats in your horse's saddle.
